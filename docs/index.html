About Me

I'm currently in my fourth year of undergrad at Colorado State University. My degree consists of a major in data science with a statistics concentration, and a minor in mathematics.

Outside of school I like to run, climb, cry during movies, and read. In fact, I love reading. A younger me would have laughed at that statement, but I have since come to realize that a well written book is the perfect vehicle for knowledge. Check out my Reading List to see what I've been reading. To get to know me on a more personal level, check out my Blog.

My preferred OS is the latest LTS release of Ubuntu. I primarily use Vim for text editing. If we all had to choose a favorite computer language mine would be C++. I'm in love with LaTeX (as everyone should be), and especially like working in TeXstudio.

In general, I plan my code before I write it, develop and test incrementally, and use search engines relentlessly without shame. Git is one of my favorite tools for obvious reasons, but mostly I just like the way it feels when I push a commit. My repos (like the one for this site) are hosted on Github.
